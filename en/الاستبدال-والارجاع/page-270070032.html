    Exchange and return



    Replacement Policy:

    The exchange with Dar Tala is very easy and convenient, and it is available for 14 days from receiving the order. All you have to do is:

    1- Contacting the customer service team on the site or sending them a message on WhatsApp 0547887072 .

    2 - Provide them with (your order number - the replacement model - the required size). To submit a replacement request for you.

    After the product arrives, inspects it and ensures that all accessories are present, the replacement order will be shipped directly.


    Exchange Terms:


    1- The product is in its original condition and has not been used, and the card on the piece has not been removed, with the full packaging and carton being provided.

    2- The customer bears the shipping cost of 50 riyals, shipping and retrieval, and if the product is defective or there is an error, Dar Thala bears the full costs.

    3- The amount paid from the shipping fees and payment fees upon receipt is not refunded to the customer.

    4- After the policy is issued by a third house , the customer must deliver the product to the nearest Aramex branch.

    5- Replacement is available only once, and the replacement request is not allowed to be returned.

    6- Products that cannot be replaced (unions, offerings, special requests products (modification).


    7- In the international exchange, the customer bears the full shipping costs and in the event of defects or errors, the customer will be compensated.


    Return Policy:


    The trip with Dar Tala is available within 3 days of receiving the order. All you have to do is:

    1- Contacting the customer service team on the site or sending them a message on WhatsApp 0548776164.

    2 - Provide us with (your order number - specifying the reason for the return - IBAN number - bank name - triple name of the account holder) to submit a return request to you.

    After the product arrives, inspected and ensures that all accessories are present, the amount will be transferred within two working days of receiving it.



    Return Terms:

    1- The product is in its original condition and has not been used, and the card on the piece has not been removed, with the full packaging and carton being provided.

    2- The customer bears the shipping cost of 50 riyals, and if the product is defective or there is an error, Dar Thala will bear the full costs.

    3- The amount paid from the shipping fees and payment fees upon receipt goes to the shipping company and cannot be returned.

    4- After the policy is issued by a third house, the customer must deliver the product to the nearest Aramex branch.

    5- Products that cannot be returned (unions, offerings, products that have been requested to be modified).

    6- In international returns, the customer bears all shipping costs and in the event of defects or errors, the customer will be compensated.
